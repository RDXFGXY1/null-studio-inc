Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cryptocurrency payments through our secure payment processor.

Is there a setup fee?

No setup fees! All plans include free setup and onboarding assistance from our team.

What happens if I cancel?

You can cancel anytime. Your service continues until the end of your billing period, and all your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation.
