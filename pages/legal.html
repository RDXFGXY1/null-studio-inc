Privacy Policy for Hinata Discord Bot

Last Updated: 19/09/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Hinata ("the Bot"), developed and operated by NullStudio Inc. ("we," "us," "our"), collects, uses, and protects information about users ("you," "your") of Discord servers that utilize the Bot.

By inviting and using Hinata, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

The Bot collects information necessary to provide its security and logging services. This includes:

  • Discord Server Information: Guild ID, server name, role names, channel names and IDs, and server settings.
  • Discord User Information: User IDs, usernames, discriminators, nicknames, and avatar URLs.
  • User-Generated Content: Message content, message edits, deleted messages, and URLs posted in channels where the Bot has read access.
  • Moderation Actions: Data related to kicks, bans, timeouts, and other administrative actions.
  • Usage Data: Data related to Bot commands used, features accessed, and API call volumes (for premium services).
  • Payment Information: For premium subscriptions, we process payments via PayPal. We do not store your full payment details. PayPal handles all payment data under its own privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of operating the Bot's features:

  • To provide and maintain our service, including security scanning, logging, and anti-nuke protection.
  • To notify server administrators of potential security threats or policy violations.
  • To manage premium subscriptions and provide paid features.
  • To generate aggregated, non-identifiable analytics on usage patterns (e.g., number of threats blocked).

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: All data is stored locally in encrypted SQLite databases (servers/database/) or JSON configuration files (servers/logs/). Each Discord server's data is isolated.
  • Security: We implement security measures to protect your data. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
  • Retention: Log data is retained according to your server's configuration and subscription tier. Data is deleted upon the Bot's removal from a server.

5. Third-Party Services

The Bot integrates with third-party services to enhance functionality:

  • PayPal: Processes all premium subscription payments. Their privacy policy governs payment data.
  • VirusTotal / URLVoid / AbuseIPDB: URLs and IPs are sent to these services for threat scanning if the feature is enabled. Their respective privacy policies apply to this data.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to our processing of your personal data.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise these rights, contact us via the support channels listed in the Bot's documentation. You can also remove all your data by removing the Bot from your server.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via:

  • Discord Support Server: https://discord.gg/8PvYKnNdXS
  • Email: null.studio.000@outlook.com
